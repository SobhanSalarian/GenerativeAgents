### Phase 2 — H5: Signed discrepancy metric
**File:** `experiment_analysis.py` — new function `compute_discrepancy()`

Formula: `discrepancy = composite_believability − coordination_validity_score`

CD validity score: `0.5 × coordination_score + 0.3 × sustainability_score + 0.2 × (1−gini)`
IC validity score: `coordination_score` alone

Gap threshold: `discrepancy > 0.20`

**Results:**
- CD: 54 gap cases. Largest: memory_planning/trial_3 (B=0.680, V=0.145, Δ=+0.535)
- IC: 10 gap cases — all IC baseline trials (B≈0.385, V=0.000, Δ≈+0.385)
- Mean discrepancy by condition (CD): baseline +0.21, memory +0.33, memory_planning +0.43, full −0.05, full_llm_reflection −0.21
- H5 supported in both scenarios

### Phase 3 — H6: Reflection saturation analysis
**File:** `experiment_analysis.py` — new function `analyze_reflection_saturation()`

Computes: total/unique counts, repetition rate, top strings, diversity curve
(cumulative unique strings vs. step), saturation step at 90% coverage.

**Results:**
- CD deterministic `full`: 3 strings, repetition=1.000, saturates at step 2
- CD LLM: 1190 strings, repetition=0.949, saturates at step 67
- IC deterministic `full`: 3 strings, repetition=0.997, saturates at step 2

### Phase 4 — H7/C4: Early-warning classifier + failure taxonomy
**New file:** `reverie/backend_server/early_warning.py`

**H7 — LOO-CV results (CD, 80 trials, label=coordination_success):**

| K | Precision | Recall | F1 | AUC |
|---|-----------|--------|-----|-----|
| 5 | 0.849 | 0.984 | 0.912 | 0.797 |
| 10 | 0.886 | 0.984 | 0.932 | 0.856 |
| 15 | 0.886 | 0.984 | 0.932 | 0.896 |

- **Lead time: K=5** — failure detectable from step 5
- **Top features:** mean_coordinated_rate, plan_citation_rate, request_ratio_slope
- **Within-condition (full, n=20, 6 failures):** AUC=0.786, P=0.429, R=0.500
  → positive signal beyond condition identity; reported as exploratory
- **Within-condition (memory_planning):** AUC=0.611

**Note on H7 interpretation:** Pooled AUC (0.919) is partly driven by condition
identity (baseline/memory/memory_planning are mostly failures). The within-condition
test (AUC=0.786 for `full`) is the honest measure — it shows modest but positive
prediction beyond condition label. Framed as exploratory per the reframing docs.

**C4 — Failure taxonomy (63 failed CD trials):**

| Type | N | Description |
|------|---|-------------|
| D: Persistent oversubscription | 38 | Heavy requests throughout; resource stressed; low believability (~0.459). Conditions: baseline, memory, memory_planning |
| E: Sub-threshold coordination failure | 25 | Just above fair share; no collapse; fails on threshold. High believability (~0.641). Conditions: full, memory_planning — the gap cases |

**Type E is the thesis-relevant pattern:** high-believability agents with modest
oversubscription that still fail coordination — directly evidences C2/H5.
